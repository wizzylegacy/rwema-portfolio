The neurosurgery team at King DRAO Hospital Kigali provides a comprehensive range of services including diagnostic tests and treatments for patients facing all types of neurological conditions. Our team of neurosurgeons specializes in treating trauma and tumors of the head and spine as well as neurovascular surgery. Neurovascular conditions treated at KFHK include but are not limited to cerebrovascular aneurysm clipping, arteriovenous malformation, brain tumors both intra-axial and extra-axial, infections and infestations including brain abscess, Neurocysticercosis (parasitic infection of the central nervous system), hydrocephalus treatment, etc. In addition, congenital conditions affecting the central nervous system are treated.
